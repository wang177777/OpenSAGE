# Public release boundaries

## Included

- Aggregate and output-level publication tables, Supplementary Data 1-3, submitted figures, and the DGQC instrument/manual.
- Source code, public analysis checks, manifests, and release documentation.

## Not included

- Historical generation inputs and evaluated document files.
- Rating-level expert records or reviewer identifiers.
- Reviewer name-to-code mappings, contact information, or free-text comments.
- Pre-unblinding/confidential system mappings beyond the public T002 table.
- Internal status workbooks, author-completion files, ethics correspondence, or submission-system credentials.
- API credentials, environment files, or retrieval caches.

Controlled rating-level and identity-bearing materials may be shared with editors and referees under appropriate confidentiality safeguards.

## Study scope

The study evaluated pre-panel drafts through document review and expert ratings. It was conducted independently of WHO.
