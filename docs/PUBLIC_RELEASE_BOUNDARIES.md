# Public release boundaries

## Included

- 56 evaluated OpenSAGE PDF drafts and 70 matched direct-generation PDF drafts.
- Locked experimental prompts and a separate corrected prospective template.
- Aggregate and output-level publication tables, Supplementary Data 1-3, submitted figures, and the DGQC instrument/manual.
- Source code, public analysis checks, manifests, and release audit documentation.

## Not included

- Rating-level expert records or reviewer identifiers.
- Reviewer name-to-code mappings, contact information, or free-text comments.
- Pre-unblinding/confidential system mappings beyond the locked public T002 table.
- Internal status workbooks, author-completion files, ethics correspondence, or submission-system credentials.
- API credentials, environment files, retrieval caches, or old Git history from the source repository.

These exclusions follow the manuscript data-availability statement: explicit public-disclosure authorization was not available for rating-level and identity-bearing materials. Controlled materials may be shared with editors/referees under appropriate confidentiality safeguards.

## Clinical-use boundary

The PDFs are evaluated draft artifacts. They have not been adopted by WHO or a guideline panel and must not be used as clinical guidance or patient-specific advice.
